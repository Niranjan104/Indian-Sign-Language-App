About ISL Converter

Empowering Communication Through Technology

Our Vision

The ISL Converter is an innovative project designed to simplify the process of converting videos into **Indian Sign Language (ISL)** representations. Our mission is to bridge the communication gap between individuals who are hearing-impaired and the broader community. We believe that technology can foster inclusivity and equal opportunities for everyone.

About Us

We are a team of passionate **Computer Science and Engineering students** from Alliance University. Driven by curiosity and innovation, we developed the ISL Converter as part of our commitment to creating technology with a social impact.
